Delivery Policy

Last updated: [LAST UPDATED DATE]

Thank you for shopping with Meridian Attire. We aim to make your shopping experience clear, smooth and reliable.

Meridian Attire is a UK-focused online boutique offering curated summer accessories for holidays, festivals and everyday styling. Orders are completed securely through our Wix store checkout and fulfilled with the support of our trusted fulfilment and shipping partners.

1. Where we deliver

We currently focus on customers in the United Kingdom.

If delivery is available to other locations, the available options and costs will be shown during checkout.

2. Order processing

After your order is placed, it is reviewed and prepared for fulfilment.

Estimated processing time: 1-3 business days

Processing times may vary during busy periods, product launches, sales, public holidays or supplier delays.

Business days are Monday to Friday, excluding UK public holidays.

3. Delivery estimates

Delivery times vary depending on the selected product, destination and fulfilment route.

Selected styles may be available with fast UK delivery. The final delivery estimate will be shown during checkout whenever available.

As a general guide, orders may arrive within: 5-15 business days for selected products

Some products may take longer depending on stock availability, fulfilment partner processing, customs checks, carrier delays or remote delivery locations.

We do not guarantee next-day delivery unless it is clearly offered for a specific product at checkout.

4. Delivery within 30 days

We aim to deliver products within 30 calendar days unless a different delivery timeframe is clearly shown or agreed at checkout.

If your order cannot be delivered within 30 calendar days and you do not wish to continue waiting, you may contact us to request a cancellation and refund, subject to your statutory rights.

5. Shipping costs

Shipping costs, if applicable, are displayed during checkout before payment is completed.

Shipping costs may vary depending on:

  • product type;
  • delivery address;
  • shipping method;
  • fulfilment location;
  • promotions or free delivery offers.

6. Tracking

Where tracking is available, tracking details will be sent by email or made available through the checkout/order confirmation process.

Please allow time for tracking information to update after your order has been dispatched.

7. Split shipments

If your order contains more than one item, products may be shipped separately. This can happen when items are fulfilled from different locations or become available at different times.

You will not be charged extra shipping for split shipments unless this is clearly shown before checkout.

8. Delays

Occasionally, delivery may be delayed due to reasons outside our control, including:

  • carrier delays;
  • high seasonal demand;
  • customs checks;
  • supplier processing delays;
  • incorrect delivery information;
  • weather disruption;
  • public holidays;
  • remote delivery addresses.

If there is a significant delay, please contact us at [CUSTOMER SUPPORT EMAIL].

9. Incorrect address

Customers are responsible for providing accurate delivery information at checkout.

If an incorrect or incomplete address is provided, delivery may be delayed, returned or unsuccessful.

Please contact us as soon as possible if you notice an address error after placing an order. We cannot guarantee that an address can be changed once the order has entered fulfilment.

10. Lost or missing orders

If your order has not arrived within the estimated timeframe, please contact us at [CUSTOMER SUPPORT EMAIL] with:

  • your full name;
  • order number;
  • delivery address;
  • any tracking information available.

We will review the issue with our fulfilment and shipping partners and provide the appropriate next step.
