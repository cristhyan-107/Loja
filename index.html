FAQ

Where do you deliver?

Meridian Attire currently focuses on customers in the United Kingdom. Available delivery options are shown during checkout.

How do I complete my purchase?

When you click Buy Now, you will be directed to our secure Wix checkout page.

Do you offer fast delivery?

Selected styles may be available with fast UK delivery. Delivery details are shown before checkout where available.

Can I return an item?

In most cases, UK online customers can tell us they wish to cancel within 14 days of receiving their item and then have another 14 days to return it. Some exceptions may apply.

What if my item arrives damaged?

Contact us as soon as possible with your order number and clear photos or video of the issue. We will review the problem and provide the appropriate next step.

Will my order arrive in separate packages?

Some orders may arrive in separate packages if items are fulfilled from different locations or become available at different times.

Are these products selected for summer?

Yes. Meridian Attire curates accessories designed for holidays, festivals and everyday summer styling.
