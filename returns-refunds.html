Returns & Refunds Policy

Last updated: [LAST UPDATED DATE]

We want you to feel confident shopping with Meridian Attire. This policy explains how returns, refunds, cancellations and damaged or incorrect items are handled.

This policy does not affect your statutory rights under UK consumer law.

1. Change-of-mind returns

If you are a consumer in the United Kingdom and purchase online, you usually have the right to cancel your order within 14 days of receiving your goods.

To use this right, you must tell us within 14 days after the day you receive your item.

After telling us you wish to cancel, you then have another 14 days to return the item.

To start a return, contact us at: [CUSTOMER SUPPORT EMAIL]

Please include:

  • your name;
  • order number;
  • item name;
  • reason for return;
  • photos if relevant.

2. Return condition

Returned items should be:

  • unused;
  • unworn;
  • in their original condition;
  • with original tags, packaging or protective materials where applicable.

You may inspect an item as you would in a physical shop, but we may reduce the refund if the item has been handled more than necessary and its value has been reduced.

3. Return shipping costs

For change-of-mind returns, customers are usually responsible for the cost of return shipping unless:

  • the item is faulty;
  • the item is incorrect;
  • the item was damaged on arrival;
  • we have agreed otherwise in writing.

We recommend using a tracked return service and keeping proof of postage.

4. Refund timing

Once your returned item is received, or once you provide evidence that it has been sent back, we will process your refund in accordance with your legal rights.

Refunds will usually be made to the original payment method.

Bank processing times may vary depending on your payment provider.

5. Original delivery cost

If you cancel the full order within the legal cancellation period, we will refund the standard delivery cost you paid, if any.

If you selected a more expensive delivery option than standard delivery, we may only refund the standard delivery amount.

6. Items that may not be returnable

Some items may not be eligible for change-of-mind returns where permitted by law, including:

  • personalised or custom-made items;
  • sealed goods that are not suitable for return for hygiene reasons if unsealed after delivery;
  • items damaged by misuse, wear or improper care.

This does not affect your rights if an item is faulty, damaged or not as described.

7. Faulty items

If an item is faulty, not as described or not fit for purpose, please contact us as soon as possible.

Under UK consumer law, you may have the right to reject faulty goods within 30 days of receiving them.

Please contact us at [CUSTOMER SUPPORT EMAIL] with:

  • your order number;
  • description of the issue;
  • clear photos or video showing the fault.

Depending on the issue, we may offer a refund, a replacement or another suitable resolution.

8. Damaged items

If your item arrives damaged, please contact us as soon as possible, ideally within 30 days of delivery.

Please include:

  • order number;
  • photos or video of the damage;
  • photos of the packaging where possible.

We will review the evidence and work with our fulfilment partners to resolve the issue.

9. Incorrect or missing items

If you receive the wrong item or an item is missing from your order, please contact us at [CUSTOMER SUPPORT EMAIL].

Please include:

  • order number;
  • photos of the item received;
  • photos of the packaging;
  • details of what is missing or incorrect.

We will review the issue and provide the appropriate next step.

10. Cancellations before dispatch

If you wish to cancel an order before it has been processed or dispatched, contact us immediately at [CUSTOMER SUPPORT EMAIL].

We will try to cancel the order, but we cannot guarantee cancellation once the order has entered fulfilment.

If cancellation is possible, a refund will be issued to the original payment method.

11. How to start a return

To start a return, email: [CUSTOMER SUPPORT EMAIL]

Subject line: Return Request - Order #[ORDER NUMBER]

Include:

  • full name;
  • order number;
  • product name;
  • reason for return;
  • photos if the item is faulty, damaged or incorrect.

We will reply with return instructions.

12. Your statutory rights

Nothing in this policy limits your statutory rights as a consumer.
