5 Accessories to Pack for a Summer Holiday

A compact packing edit for sunny days, city breaks and warm evenings.

Start with one silk-style scarf, one pair of oversized sunglasses and a lightweight jewellery detail. These pieces take very little space but can change the mood of simple summer outfits.

The packing list

  • A scarf for hair, neck or bag styling.
  • Oversized sunglasses for travel days.
  • Raffia earrings for evening texture.
  • A straw clutch for dinners and beach plans.
  • Pearl clips for quick polished hair.
