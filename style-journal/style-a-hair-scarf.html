How to Style a Hair Scarf This Summer

Three easy scarf ideas that feel considered without looking overdone.

Tie your scarf as a headband for travel days, wrap it around a low bun for evenings, or knot it around a straw bag to add colour and texture.

Style notes

  • Keep jewellery minimal if the scarf is the focus.
  • Pair silk-style textures with clean sunglasses.
  • Use crochet textures for festival outfits.
