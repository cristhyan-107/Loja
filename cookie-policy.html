This Cookie Policy explains how Meridian Attire uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function, remember preferences and understand how visitors use the site.

2. Types of cookies we may use

Strictly necessary cookies: These cookies are needed for the website to work properly. They may support basic functions such as security, page navigation, cookie preferences and access to external checkout links.

Analytics cookies: These help us understand how visitors use the website, such as which pages are viewed and how users interact with products. We only use these where permitted and, where required, with your consent.

Marketing cookies: These may be used to measure advertising performance or support personalised marketing. We only use these where permitted and, where required, with your consent.

Preference cookies: These help remember choices such as cookie settings or display preferences.

3. Third-party cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services, including:

  • analytics providers;
  • advertising platforms;
  • Wix checkout or store pages;
  • embedded social media features.

Third-party services may process data according to their own privacy and cookie policies.

4. Cookie consent

When required, we will ask for your consent before placing non-essential cookies on your device.

You should be able to:

  • accept cookies;
  • reject non-essential cookies;
  • manage preferences.

5. Managing cookies

You can also control cookies through your browser settings.

If you block all cookies, some parts of the website may not work correctly.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time.

The latest version will be posted on this page.
